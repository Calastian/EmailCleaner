Loads your disposable/spam host list from hosts.csv
Scans all mail in Inbox
Classifies into Low / Med / High
Moves out of Inbox by copying to the label folder and deleting from Inbox (then expunging)
Treats any no-reply/noreply/donotreply sender as Low
Treats any sender whose domain is in hosts.csv as Low
Defaults unknown senders to Med, and “person-like” domains / high keywords to High
